About

Welcome to my website. My name is Tyler Russell and I am a recent math graduate from University of Texas at Tyler. I love programming, reading, running, and the Spanish language. I am currently searching for programming jobs in the United States with a specialization in backend programming.

My interest in programming began in high school when a friend started teaching me. Since then I have not stopped programming and continue to enjoy it every day. My main interests lie in computation and simulation but I have started to dabble into the field of data science and computer vision.

I have a broad skillset that includes backend technologies like Python, Flask, Pandas, and SQL as well as exposure to frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Bootstrap.
